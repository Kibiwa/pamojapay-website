What PamojaPay does

Everything your SACCO needs, nothing it doesn't

💰

Savings Management

Track membership fees, monthly savings, welfare, shares and voluntary deposits per member. Real-time totals, contribution history and reminders.

🏦

Loan Register

Flat and reducing balance loans, automatic repayment schedules, overdue tracking and 2% monthly penalty calculation. Full NPL reporting.

📊

Finance Engine

Live cash-in-bank balance, capital gains pool, AGM dividend distribution, expense tracking and investment management — all reconciled automatically.

👥

Member Portal

Each member logs in to see their own savings, loan balances, contribution history and download statements. Secure, mobile-first.

📋

4-Step Approvals

Treasurer → Finance Manager → Admin → Auditor. Every loan and member addition goes through a proper approval chain with full audit trail.

📄

PDF Documents

Professional loan agreements, payment receipts, member statements, share certificates and overdue penalty reports — generated instantly.
