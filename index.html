About PamojaPay

The name says it all

“Pamoja” means “Together” in Swahili — and that spirit of togetherness is at the heart of every cooperative. SACCOs exist because people come together to pool resources, support each other, and build collective wealth.

We built Pamoja Pay because we saw too many SACCOs across Uganda and Africa struggling with paper records, spreadsheets, and systems not built for the cooperative context. Members could not check their own balances. Loan approvals dragged on. Audits were painful and manual.

Pamoja Pay is the answer — a complete, modern platform built specifically for how cooperatives in Uganda and across Africa actually work.

Our mission: To digitize and empower cooperative societies across Uganda and Africa.
