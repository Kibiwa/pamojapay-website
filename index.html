FAQ

Common questions

What is Pamoja Pay?▼
Pamoja Pay is a complete SACCO management platform built for cooperative societies in Uganda and Africa. It handles everything from member onboarding to loan management, governance, and reporting in one web-based system accessible from any device.
How does pricing work?▼
Three simple components: (1) Base License — UGX 500,000 one-time, mandatory for every SACCO. (2) Feature Modules — UGX 300,000 each, one-time, completely optional. Buy only what you need, add more any time, own forever. (3) Monthly Fee — UGX 2,000 per member per month. No per-transaction fees. No hidden costs.
What is included in the base system?▼
The Base License (UGX 500,000 one-time) includes: member management, savings tracking, member self-service portal, basic reports and PDF statements, 500 email/SMS/WhatsApp credits per month, and role-based staff access with audit log. It does NOT include loan management, expenses, investments, voting, or other advanced features — those require purchasing the relevant optional modules.
Can we start with base and add modules later?▼
Yes. Add any feature module for UGX 300,000 one-time whenever you are ready. Your member data stays completely intact. Your monthly fee only changes when your member count changes.
Are there any per-transaction fees?▼
No. Never. The monthly fee (UGX 2,000 x members) is the only recurring cost. There are zero per-transaction fees regardless of how many loans are processed, payments recorded, or reports generated.
Is there a contract?▼
Month-to-month with no long-term commitment. Cancel anytime. We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee if you are not satisfied after going live.
Can members access from their phones?▼
Yes. The member portal works on any smartphone browser with no app download needed. Members log in securely using their Member ID and password.
What happens when internet goes down?▼
If you purchase the Offline Mode module (UGX 300,000 one-time), Pamoja Pay works fully offline. Staff continue all operations normally and changes sync automatically when connectivity returns.
Is our data secure?▼
Yes. Bank-level encryption, row-level security, daily automated backups, and an immutable audit trail. Your data belongs to your SACCO and we never share or sell it.
What if we have more than 5,000 members?▼
Contact us for a custom quote. The UGX 2,000 per member per month still applies, but we can tailor the one-time setup based on your specific scale and needs.

About PamojaPay

The name says it all

“Pamoja” means “Together” in Swahili — and that spirit of togetherness is at the heart of every cooperative. SACCOs exist because people come together to pool resources, support each other, and build collective wealth.

We built Pamoja Pay because we saw too many SACCOs across Uganda and Africa struggling with paper records, spreadsheets, and systems not built for the cooperative context. Members could not check their own balances. Loan approvals dragged on. Audits were painful and manual.

Pamoja Pay is the answer — a complete, modern platform built specifically for how cooperatives in Uganda and across Africa actually work.

Our mission: To digitize and empower cooperative societies across Uganda and Africa.
